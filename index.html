Recent News

"Bangladesh 2.0" refers to the nation's transformative phase following the August 2024 political upheaval, which led to the ousting of Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina and the installation of Nobel laureate Muhammad Yunus as interim leader. This period is characterized by significant reforms aimed at establishing a more democratic and inclusive society.

Bangladesh Launches New Satelite

Time:10:25 PM Date:05/01/2025

Bangladesh's journey into space began with the successful launch of Bangabandhu-1, its first geostationary communications satellite, on May 12, 2018. Launched from Kennedy Space Center in Florida aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket, this milestone marked Bangladesh as the 57th nation to operate a satellite.

Bangladesh: Floods and Landslides

Time:10:30 PM Date:05/01/2025

In 2024, Bangladesh experienced severe flooding that caused significant loss of life and widespread displacement. In August, heavy rainfall and water surges from upstream regions affected 11 districts, resulting in at least 71 fatalities and impacting approximately 5.8 million people.
